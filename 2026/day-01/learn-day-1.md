# Day 01 - My 90 Days DevOps Learning Plan

## Current Level

I am a fresher who is currently learning Linux, Git, and DevOps fundamentals. I am starting my journey to build a career in Cloud and DevOps Engineering.

## My Understanding of DevOps & Cloud Engineering

DevOps is a culture and set of practices that combines Development and Operations to deliver software faster, more reliably, and with automation.

Cloud Engineering is the process of designing, deploying, and managing applications and infrastructure on cloud platforms like AWS.

## Why I Am Learning DevOps & Cloud

* To build a successful career in IT as a DevOps Engineer.
* To gain hands-on experience with modern tools and cloud technologies.
* To improve my automation and problem-solving skills.

## My 3 Goals for the Next 90 Days

1. Build strong fundamentals in Linux, Git, Networking, and Shell Scripting.
2. Learn AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD tools through hands-on practice.
3. Deploy a real-world application using Docker, Kubernetes, and a CI/CD pipeline.

## 3 Core DevOps Skills I Want to Build

1. Linux administration and troubleshooting.
2. CI/CD pipeline creation and automation.
3. Kubernetes deployment and debugging.

## Weekly Time Budget

* Weekdays: 2-3 hours per day.
* Weekends: 4-6 hours per day.

## How I Will Stay Consistent

* Study and practice every day without skipping.
* Maintain notes and document my learning.
* Share daily progress publicly on LinkedIn and GitHub.
* Complete hands-on labs and mini projects regularly.

## Where I Want to Reach

By the end of these 90 days, I want to become confident in DevOps fundamentals, complete real-world projects, and be ready to apply for DevOps and Cloud Engineer roles.
